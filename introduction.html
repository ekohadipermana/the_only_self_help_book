Introduction

The Hope Theory

Your basic belief about yourself and your world is the main determinant for your success in life.

Your thinking produces beliefs. Your beliefs produce hope. Your hope determine your attitude.

Your attitude determine your behaviour. Your behaviour changes your performance.

Your performance changes your life.

So you can change the images in your mind about yourself to start the changes.

In general, your belief about yourself and your world is unreliable, it needs reassessment!

You can THROW your outdated thinking and replace it with a new FRESH one.

If what you want in the future is different with your current reality, you must CHANGE what you are doing NOW!

Focus on challenges and opportunities, not on problem and shortcoming.

Positive thinking is not letting that 10% imperfection affecting 100% of your thinking.

Positive thinking focus to the positive aspect in your life, in every event, using positive action, words and images.
